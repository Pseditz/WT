DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information Technology is the fastest growing technologies in our culture today. One of the ramifications of that growth is a dramatic increase in the number of professions where an understanding of various technologies is essential for success and a proportionate increase in the number and type of students taking courses to learn about them. Our institution focuses in spending every precious moment in maintaining high standards by revising the syllabi to suit the high industrial requirements. The department offers sound and versatile courses for the award of B. Tech degree. An excellent team of faculty members moulds the enthusiastic students to form a very strong software industry to rule the future.
